About the Store

Hello and welcome to our bookstore.
This is not just a store — it’s a journey through reading, a space for thought, work, and critique.

The store is designed for those who are passionate about politics, current affairs, history, dystopian literature, and other non-fiction works.

We, the administrators of this store, invite you to explore a selection of books we chose specifically for readers like us.

I’m Sagi Akshikar, the creator of this website, and I dedicate it to those who are just beginning their reading journey as well as to experienced readers looking for inspiration.

Our goal is to make this site accessible to a wide audience who shares our love for books.

Every book featured on this site is one I, Sagi Akshikar, have read and found worth sharing. The site links to online purchase options e-vrit.co.il, a well-known Israeli platform for buying books.

Happy reading!
